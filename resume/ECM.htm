Emergency Control Monitor(ECM)

Network monitor app for an academic project. Tests emergency sensors at set intervals and on user prompt.
Functionality includes sensors that can be added manually or automatically via a network-scan, historical reports that can be saved to pdf, and can be sent to an emailing-list.

My role:

- Lead developer, also responsible for monitoring and reporting subsystems.

- Scrum master - maintaining backlog, organising scrum and sprint planning meetings with minutes, gantt charts and monitoring risks.
